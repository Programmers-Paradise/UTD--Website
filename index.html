Vision / Mission and Goals

To create ethically sound engineers to lead in industry and academia, nationally and internationally, meeting the demands of society in the area of Computer Sciences while being the epitome of research and innovation in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence.

Our mission is to offer world-class education, research guidance, professional consultancy, and manpower training, leading in Computer Science Engineering.
